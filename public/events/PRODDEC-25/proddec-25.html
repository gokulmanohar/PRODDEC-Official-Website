Introduction

About this event
PRODDEC@25 is a 2-day event organized by the Product Design and Development Centre, College of Engineering Chengannur. This event is conducted to celebrate the 25th year of PRODDEC CEC. The event comprises a total of 3 sessions spanning over 2 days. The first day has two sessions Introduction To Python Programming - A walk through the basics of Python, by Arjun V, S8 Electrical student & Phase To Face - A brief introduction to Oscilloscopes, handled by Mithun Zacharia, S8 student. On the second day, our closest alumnus Muhammed Rabeeh will lead a full-day session titled Warm-Up - Familiarisation and applications of solar energy and panels.

Introduction To Python Programming
Phase To Face - A Brief Introduction To Oscilloscopes
Familiarization And Applications Of Solar Energy And Panels
